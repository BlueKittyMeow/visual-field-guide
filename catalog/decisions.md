## Initial decisions

### 2026-07-15 — Uncanny as overlay

**Decision:** Core structural principle

**What works:** The base identity remains scholarly, archival, specimen-oriented, domestic, handmade, optical, and fin-de-siècle. Darkness and degradation can then alter an already coherent world.

**What does not:** Treating creepiness, decay, or generic darkness as the identity itself.

**What this teaches the field guide:** Separate identity from effect. The uncanny should reveal the archive becoming unreliable, not replace the archive.

### 2026-07-15 — Shell size boxes

**Decision:** Core autobiographical reference

**What works:** Cigar-box-sized shell collections sorted by size, wrapped in brown kraft paper, with the size lettered on the lid. This joins domestic memory, taxonomy, modest materials, handwriting, and specimen culture in one object.

**What does not:** Polished luxury collector cases or decorative coastal styling.

**What this teaches the field guide:** Personal systems of sorting and keeping are more important than generic museum spectacle.
